Welcome to Barangay Diri Maturakan!

The Barangay Portal is a single window uniting all web-based Barangay content to maximize efficiency and provide rapid, high-quality service to citizens. As part of the plan of the Barangay Diri Maturakan to slowly transition all the business processes and transactions online, various application forms for individual,certification and others use are now available for download online. Barangay is committed to providing its constituents with an easy, fast, and hassle-free transaction experience.
